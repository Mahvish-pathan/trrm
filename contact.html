Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ask a farming question?

Simply go to our "Ask Query" page, select the appropriate category (Pest, Crop, Weather, or Market), and type your question. You'll get an instant response.

Is the service free to use?

Yes, our basic advisory service is completely free for all farmers. We believe in supporting the farming community with accessible information.

How accurate is the information provided?

All our information is curated by agricultural experts and based on proven farming practices. However, we recommend consulting with local agricultural officers for specific cases.

Can I get weather updates for my specific location?

Currently, we provide general weather information. We're working on location-specific weather updates in future versions.
