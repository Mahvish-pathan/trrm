About Krishi Assistant

Empowering farmers with reliable agricultural guidance

Our Mission

At Krishi Assistant, we are dedicated to supporting farmers with reliable, accessible, and practical agricultural guidance. Our platform provides instant answers to common farming questions, helping farmers make informed decisions about pest control, crop care, weather planning, and market strategies.

What We Offer

  • Pest Control Solutions: Expert advice on identifying and managing various pests that affect crops
  • Crop Care Guidance: Best practices for cultivation, fertilization, and harvesting
  • Weather Information: Current weather updates and forecasts to help plan farming activities
  • Market Price Updates: Real-time market prices and trends for informed selling decisions
